What Is Microneedling?

Microneedling — also known as collagen induction therapy — is a minimally invasive skin treatment procedure that uses ultra-fine needles to create controlled micro-channels in the skin's surface. These tiny punctures activate the body's natural healing process, boosting collagen stimulation and elastin production. The result is smoother, firmer, and more radiant skin over time.
